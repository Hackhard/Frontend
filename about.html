Prayukti 2020

The annual techno management festival of Haldia Institute of Technology celebrating its 19th anniversary is getting bigger and better. It boasts of various events ranging from robotics to movie making and from junkyard arts to discussing world politics. Scheduled from 2nd to 5th March, the stage will be set for innovations and competitions. Bring your ideas forward, create, compete and win!
