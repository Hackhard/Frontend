Introduction

Transformers? checkbox ticked. The noble autobots and devious deceptions; two intergalactic races of robots crash-land on earth and battle. Have you ever wondered how a battle between two such extremely powerful futuristic robots would look like? Prayukti presents Battle Ground,an event to unleash your best robots to battle for glory. It doesn't matter if you are an amateur or a professional roboteer operating their own controlled robots to fight against each other, whilst avoiding arena hazards. Collect your components, plan your schemes, calibrate your sensors and gear up to witness the robowars. Your brute strength and cat-like reflexes hold your key to success.
