Introduction

Have you seen white streaks across the blue sky and wondered about rockets? Have you seen a pressure cooker's whistle blow off? And do you know the result of both of these phenomenas coalescing? Water rockets!   Prayukti 2k19 presents Dive Bomber - an event in which water rockets built by students will claim the landscape.Sleek aerodynamic rockets shooting up high and far with a jet of water trailing them,a feast for the eyes.Design and strength of the rockets will reign superior, so hurry up and start making your own. The competition will be based on how well your water rocket performs and how much ground it covers. Exciting prizes wait on the other side of the launch-pad, get pumpin'.
