Introduction

Those colorful bullets spluttering out of the gun and teams strengthening up, knocking down opponents with head shots, being meticulous enough not to being hit by one, is indeed a great pick to brush up the ebullience. Paintball is a competitive team shooting sport in which players eliminate opponents from play by hitting them with spherical dye-filled gelatin capsules ("paintballs") that break upon impact.A game field is scattered with natural or artificial terrain, which players use for tactical cover.
