Introduction

Bouncy and edgy, featuring gritty dubstep elements, building atmospheres, and punchy synth drums that create an energetic and determined mood. Prayukti brings you LAKSHYA. A platform for self-driving and manually controlled cars. Roborace, a competition with wirelessly driving and battery-powered vehicles, used to scamper through a plethora of obstructions. Nowhere else will you enjoy such unrestricted access to machines and the drivers who made them famous. An event where the direction is more important than speed. An event where the sole purpose is not pushing or flipping but it's all about speed. Immerse yourself into the entertainment of the festival of speed. Gear up, calibrate your electronic equipments, strengthen your feline reflexes and witness the ceaseless amusement.
