Introduction

Ever thought of making someone carry out a desired task? Well, it might be an ordeal in reality, but training a teensy autonomous robot, in the vast paradigm of Robotics, is as easy as pie. Have you ever wondered how an autobot could be made to follow dichromatic lines with the assistance of just a few electronic equipments? Well, good news for the zealous minds! Prayukti 2019 presents Track-O-Bot. Assemble the sensors, microcontrollers, hardware parts and other equipments to prepare the hastiest and the most intellectual bot ever, through coding! Construct such a bot which follows the white and black lines with sheer focus and may the most scintillating bot acheive victory!
